Let me begin by introducing myself first. My name is Vince Enriquez and I am 19 years old. I immigrated to Canada in 2012 after spending the first 11 years of my life in the Philippines. During my leisure/free time, I usually spend it by browsing the internet, watching videos, playing video games, and watching live sports on TV (especially basketball and football). In terms of my previous school experience, I graduated from elementary school in 2014 and graduated from high school in 2019. I have had some previous post-secondary experience in Vancouver Community College (VCC). in the previous year, as I took Anatomy and Physiology and tried to pursue a career in the medical field before I decided to change course and instead pursue a career in Information Technology by taking the two-year CIT diploma program in BCIT. The factors that caused me to choose BCIT as a post-secondary institution of choice is that it will help me to explore the world of computers and technology in further detail so that I am able to have the tools and resources I need to pursue a successful work career in computers and information systems in the future, especially in work companies such as Microsoft.

During the summertime, me and my family usually likes to go out on different places and different countries. Some of the places that I have visited during the summer include my home country of the Philippines in 2016, the city of Los Angeles in 2015, 2018, and 2019 to visit my cousins from my dad's side of the family, and the city of Portland and the Oregon coast in 2014.

Currently, my mom (born in 1968) works in Downtown Vancouver, my dad (born in 1966) works in Richmond, and my older brother (born in 1999) also studies in BCIT in the Electrical Engineering program. My brother is planning to graduate in 2023, while I am planning to graduate in late 2022 or possibly 2023 as well.
